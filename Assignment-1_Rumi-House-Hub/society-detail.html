Namal Environmental Club

Social Impact

Our Mission

NEC runs plantation drives on and around campus, leads clean-up and waste-management campaigns, and promotes recycling and sustainable practices to support the idea of a "Green Namal." The club organizes awareness sessions and projects so students can participate directly in environmental conservation and responsible campus living.

Key Activities & Responsibilities

Through campus-wide coordination, the Namal Environmental Club targets key areas of co-curricular engagement:

  • Execute bi-annual plantation drives on campus premises and nearby communities.
  • Develop efficient waste segregation and active recycling processes within student residential halls.
  • Conduct interactive ecological seminars and collaborative campus cleanup drives.

Executive Body Structure

Student representatives handling visual and operational coordination under the supervision of the department:

  • Executive Lead President
  • Society Coordinator Vice President
  • General Secretary Student Officer
  • Media Manager Student Coordinator
