Annual Inter-House Sports Gala

Organized by the Sports & Adventure Club in collaboration with Rumi House.

Activity Overview

The Annual Inter-House Sports Gala ensures that all students, regardless of gender, take part in physical drills and recreational sports through inter-house matches and competitions, helping them balance academics with physical wellbeing. Rumi House works in active collaboration with house masters to organize the boating trips, trekking expeditions, and field sports that highlight the academic year.

Athletes from Rumi House will compete in running sprints, indoor chess tournaments, archery matches, and the iconic Indus River boating sprint. Gold points accumulated in these segments will determine the final semester house sports trophy.
