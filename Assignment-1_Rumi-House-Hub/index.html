Discover, Connect, and Lead in Rumi House.

Rumi House is the central office for all student societies and clubs at Namal University, headed by the Chief Patron. It provides a dedicated space where society representatives can hold meetings, rehearsals, planning sessions and discussions before events, helping them turn ideas into well-organized activities that represent their clubs and the university. [namal.edu]

The Rumi House team monitors the progress of each society and club, collaborates with them on events, and also initiates its own sports and adventure activities to positively engage both faculty and staff. [namal.edu]

An editorial board under Rumi House regularly publishes the Rumi Newsletter, documenting major events, visits and society activities across the academic year and sharing highlights from life at Namal. [namal.edu]

About Rumi House

Student societies at Namal give students structured platforms to collaborate, innovate and give back to the university community. [friendsofnamalusa]

Through Rumi House, these societies receive administrative support, space and guidance so they can organize talks, competitions, cultural festivals, sports events, community service projects and literary activities that enrich campus life. [namal.edu]

Rumi House also oversees eligibility criteria and shortlisting processes for society executive bodies, defines the roles of patrons and the Patron‑in‑Chief, and can suspend memberships when policies are violated, ensuring societies remain active, accountable and inclusive. [namal.edu]

Society Highlights

A CSS Grid 3×3 layout showcasing nine active societies at Rumi House.

Namal Environmental Club (NEC)

Runs plantation drives on and around campus, leads clean-up and waste-management campaigns, and promotes recycling and sustainable practices to support the idea of a “Green Namal.” [friendsofnamalusa]

#Environment

Namal Idea Club (NIC)

Provides a platform for students to explore new ideas, problem-solving and innovation, especially around entrepreneurship and creative projects. [friendsofnamalusa]

#Innovation

Namal Society for Social Impact (NSSI)

Focuses on community service and social-impact initiatives, encouraging Namalites to design and execute projects that benefit local communities and address real social challenges. [friendsofnamalusa]

#SocialImpact

Namal Literary & Debating Society (LDS)

Promotes literature, reading and creative writing, and helps aspiring writers discover and polish their talents by publishing their work and organizing workshops and seminars. [namal.edu]

#Literature

Namal Sports & Adventure Club (NSAC)

Ensures that all students, regardless of gender, take part in physical drills and recreational sports through inter-house matches and competitions, helping balance academics with physical wellbeing. [namal.edu]

#Sports

Namal Dramatic Club (NDC)

Focuses on theatrical performances, scriptwriting, and acting, giving students a creative outlet to express narratives, host plays, and entertain the campus community through expressive arts.

#Drama

Namal Media Club (VoN)

Acts as the “eyes and ears” of campus life by covering events on and off campus and promoting a positive image of Namal on social media, teaching photography and videography. [namal.edu]

#Media

Skills Development Society (SDS)

Dedicated to enhancing professional and technical skills through practical workshops, training sessions, and peer-to-peer learning opportunities aimed at career readiness.

#Skills

How to Register & Why Join

Steps to Register (Ordered List)

  1. Open Rumi House Hub in your browser
  2. Browse upcoming events and society listings
  3. Select the society or event you like
  4. Fill in the membership / registration form below
  5. Upload your profile picture (optional)
  6. Click Submit and check your email for confirmation

Benefits of Joining (Unordered List)

  • Networking with seniors, peers, and industry professionals
  • Leadership and teamwork experience on real projects
  • Skill development through focused workshops
  • Confidence through stage, speaking, and pitching opportunities
  • Official certificates and portfolio-worthy achievements
  • Lasting friendships and vibrant community at campus events
