Discover Our Vision

Rumi House is the central office for all student societies and clubs at Namal University, headed by the Chief Patron. It provides a dedicated space where society representatives can hold meetings, rehearsals, planning sessions and discussions before events, helping them turn ideas into well-organized activities that represent their clubs and the university.

The Rumi House team monitors the progress of each society and club, collaborates with them on events, and also initiates its own sports and adventure activities to positively engage both faculty and staff. An editorial board under Rumi House regularly publishes the Rumi Newsletter, documenting major events, visits and society activities across the academic year and sharing highlights from life at Namal.

"Building leadership, creative expression, and sportsmanship across the student body under a centralized digital network."
