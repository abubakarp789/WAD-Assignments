Rumi House Identity & Vision

Foster Excellence, Brotherhood & Character

Rumi House, named after the prominent Sufi poet Jalal-ad-Din Muhammad Rumi, represents academic excellence, spiritual synthesis, deep compassion, and continuous learning among Namal University students.

The Rumi House Council Vision

Our council organizes academic seminars, peer tutoring projects, local social-impact outreach drives, and sports challenges to foster holistic student development.
